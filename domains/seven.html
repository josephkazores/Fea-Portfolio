Domain 7: Personal Growth and Professional Development

Continue

This domain made me reflect deeply on who I am and who I want to become as a teacher. Throughout my field study, I gained confidence, learned from my mentors, and saw the importance of continuous self-improvement.

I learned that teaching is not a one-time skill but a lifelong commitment to growth. Every experience whether observing, assisting, or reflecting, helped me see how I can be better. I also discovered the importance of humility and openness to feedback, as they lead to personal and professional growth.

As I move forward in my journey, I promise to keep learning, to stay passionate, and to use my knowledge and heart to inspire my future students.
