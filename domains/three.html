Domain 3: Diversity of Learners

Continue

In every class, there are students with different learning styles, personalities, and backgrounds. During my field study, I saw how some students learn quickly while others need more time and attention. Some are confident, while others are shy and quiet.

I realized that teachers must be sensitive to these differences and adjust their teaching accordingly. I observed teachers using group work, visual aids, and even games to make sure everyone understands the lesson in their own way. I learned that inclusivity and fairness are essential parts of good teaching.

As a future teacher, I want to treat all students equally while also recognizing their unique strengths and challenges. I will use strategies that give every learner a chance to participate, succeed, and feel valued.
